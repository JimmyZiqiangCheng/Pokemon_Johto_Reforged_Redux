# Boss Battles

Boss battles are the curated high-importance trainer records imported in Step
4: Gym Leaders, Elite Four, Champions, Red, major rivals, Rocket leaders, and
late-postgame challenge records already present in the trainer table.

## Current Audit

- Boss records in the Perfect Johto audit: 81.
- Gym Leaders: all leader-class records have six Pokemon.
- Elite Four: all Elite Four records have six Pokemon.
- Champions/Red: all Champion-class and Red records have six Pokemon.
- Major rival fights from mid-game onward have six Pokemon where appropriate.
- Major Rocket Executive/Rocket Boss records at level 35+ have six Pokemon.

## Remaining Risk

Static data validation and build success do not prove route pacing. Manual
playtesting is still needed for local difficulty spikes, optional trainer
density, Rocket/Silver pacing, rematches, Red, and late-postgame unlock timing.
