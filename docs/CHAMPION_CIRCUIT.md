# Champion Circuit

The Saffron Fighting Dojo Champion Circuit exposes repeatable late-postgame
battles using the Phase 4 trainer records already imported from Perfect Johto.

## Unlocks

- Lance and Blue unlock after all 16 badges.
- Red, Steven, Wallace, and Cynthia unlock after the original Mt. Silver Red
  defeat flag.

## Battles

- Lance: trainer 733, levels 92-96.
- Blue: trainer 727, levels 92-96.
- Red: trainer 260, levels 92-96.
- Steven: trainer 738, levels 92-96.
- Wallace: trainer 739, levels 92-96.
- Cynthia: trainer 740, levels 92-96.
