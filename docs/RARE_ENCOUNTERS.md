# Rare Encounters

Rare finds are curated low-rate placements inside the ordinary wild encounter
tables.

They are already combined into the regular encounter data. Disabling the
random roaming legendary overlay would not remove rare-find Pokemon, because
these species are direct entries in `Encounters.c` and `SafariEncounters.c`.

## Slot Rules

- Primary land rares use slot 8 at 4%.
- Curated secondary land rares may use slot 9 at 4%.
- Surf rares use slot 3 at 4%.
- Fishing rares use slot 4, now changed from 5% to 4%.
- Rock Smash is not used for rare finds because its slot structure is 80%/20%.

## Placement Rules

- Meaningful non-Safari areas receive one to three rare species.
- Rare species are reserved for strong current forms, lines whose final form
  reaches 500+ BST, or approved regional forms.
- Rare placements include Perfect Johto's early and semantic access such as
  early Houndour, Teddiursa, Ponyta, Hisuian Sneasel, Paldean Wooper, Alolan
  Geodude, Galarian Zigzagoon, Lapras, Kangaskhan, Tauros, and sparse
  pseudo-legendary initial forms.
